## Brand & Style
The design system is rooted in the philosophy of "Botanical Minimalism" infused with **Vedic Astrological Color Harmony**. The palette draws intentional balance between the energies of **Mercury (Budha)** and **Venus (Shukra)** — two of the most influential grahas in Vedic astrology — to create an aesthetic that is intellectually vibrant yet emotionally nurturing.

### ☿ Mercury (Budha) — The Intellect
Mercury governs intellect, communication, commerce, and growth. Its color is **green** — representing vitality, learning, and adaptability. The Mercury green (`#3D7A4F`) energizes primary actions, brand presence, and interactive elements, stimulating the mind and encouraging engagement.

### ♀ Venus (Shukra) — The Heart
Venus governs love, beauty, harmony, art, and luxury. Her colors are **warm cream, pure white, and soft rose** (`#FFF8F0`, `#FFFFFF`, `#D4A0A0`). Venus cream forms the canvas of the entire experience, while Venus rose (`#D4A0A0`) accents love-related elements like wishlist hearts, special badges, and premium highlights. Together they create a nurturing, harmonious atmosphere.

### The Balance
Mercury without Venus becomes cold and analytical. Venus without Mercury becomes overly emotional without direction. Their balance — **green intellect + cream/rose heart** — creates an experience that is both smart and beautiful, stimulating and calming, premium and approachable.

The visual style is characterized by:
- **Minimalism:** Massive whitespace and a "breathing" layout that emphasizes plant photography.
- **Organic Precision:** A blend of geometric perfection in UI elements with the organic fluidity of nature.
- **Calmness:** A low-friction interface that evokes the serenity of a greenhouse.
- **Tactile Softness:** Smooth transitions and rounded forms that feel approachable and high-end.

## Colors
The palette is a sophisticated "Mercury-Venus Balance" spectrum. 

- **Foundational Neutrals (Venus):** Warm Cream (`#FFF8F0`) provides a canvas that is softer and more nurturing than pure white, while Venus Surface tones create subtle depth for section alternating without breaking the airy flow.
- **The Greens (Mercury):** Mercury Green (`#3D7A4F`) is used for primary actions and brand presence. It represents intellectual vitality and growth.
- **Venus Accent (Rose):** Venus Rose (`#D4A0A0`) is reserved for love, beauty, and heart-centered elements — wishlist hearts, premium badges, and special highlights. It must be used sparingly to maintain its emotional impact.
- **Premium Accents (Gold):** Warm Gold (`#C49A44`) is used for star ratings, "Premium Collection" badges, and limited edition labels.
- **Typography:** Deep charcoal (`#2A2A2A`) is used instead of pure black to maintain a softer, more organic contrast ratio.

## Typography
The typographic hierarchy relies on the **organically sculpted** forms of **Fraunces** for headings and the **utilitarian clarity** of **Inter** for body text — an earthy, warm pairing that feels handmade yet modern.

- **Headlines (Fraunces):** Use generous bottom margins. Fraunces features a "soft-serve" serif structure with optical size variation, giving headings an organic, handcrafted warmth that mirrors the brand's botanical roots.
- **Body (Inter):** Inter is set with a slightly increased line height (1.6) to ensure the interface feels spacious and easy to navigate. Its crisp, functional letterforms provide excellent readability at all sizes.
- **Labels:** Small labels used for plant care (e.g., "Low Light", "Pet Friendly") should use the `label-sm` style with increased tracking for maximum legibility at small sizes.

## Layout & Spacing
This design system employs a **Fluid Grid** with fixed maximum constraints. 

- **The 8px Rhythm:** All padding and margins must be multiples of 8px to maintain mathematical harmony.
- **Sectioning:** Vertical breathing room is prioritized. Standard section gaps are 120px on desktop to allow the brand imagery to "own" the viewport.
- **Grid:** A 12-column grid is used for desktop. For the product gallery, a 3-column or 4-column layout is preferred to ensure plant images are large enough to show leaf detail.
- **Responsive Behavior:** On mobile, margins shrink to 20px, and section gaps reduce to 64px. Product grids reflow to a 2-column or 1-column layout depending on the complexity of the information.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth is created through **Ambient Shadows** and **Tonal Layering** rather than heavy borders.

- **Level 1 (Base):** Warm Ivory background.
- **Level 2 (Cards/Surface):** Pure White with a very soft, multi-layered shadow (0px 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.04)).
- **Level 3 (Interactive/Hover):** When a user hovers over a product card, the shadow should deepen (0px 12px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)) and the element should lift by 4px.
- **Overlays (Sticky Header):** A frosted glass effect (Backdrop Blur: 20px) combined with a 1px bottom border in `#DCE8DC`. 
- **Transitions:** All elevation changes must use a 300ms `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` transition for a "heavy" but smooth feel.

## Shapes
The shape language is defined by the **18px radius (rounded-lg)**.

- **Primary Containers:** Product cards, category tiles, and modal windows must use 18px corners.
- **Small Elements:** Buttons and input fields use a slightly smaller 12px radius to appear more precise while remaining cohesive.
- **Visual Continuity:** Image containers inside cards must inherit the 18px radius on top corners to maintain a nested, "fitted" appearance.
- **Iconography:** Use rounded-end caps (Soft/Round) for all stroke-based icons to match the UI's friendliness.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Forest Green background, White text. 300ms transition on hover to Deep Green.
- **Secondary:** Transparent background, Forest Green 1.5px border.
- **Interaction:** Incorporate a subtle "scale-down" effect (0.98) on click/active states to simulate physical feedback.

### Product Cards
- **Image:** Features a subtle "Inner Glow" to prevent images with white backgrounds from bleeding into the card.
- **Hover:** Triggers a 1.05x image zoom (overflow hidden) and the Level 3 elevation described in the Elevation section.
- **Badges:** Small, pill-shaped tags (e.g., "New", "Eco-friendly") placed in the top-left corner with 50% opacity backgrounds.

### Announcement Bar
- **Style:** Deep Green background with White `label-md` text. 
- **Animation:** Should be fixed at the very top, pushing the header down.

### Input Fields
- **Design:** Pure White background with a 1px `#DCE8DC` border. On focus, the border transitions to Forest Green and gains a 4px Soft Sage "outer glow" (box-shadow).

### Lists & Navigation
- **Header:** Sticky with `backdrop-filter: blur(15px)`. Uses a 1px divider rather than a shadow for a cleaner look.
- **Links:** Simple text with a 2px underline that grows from the center on hover.
