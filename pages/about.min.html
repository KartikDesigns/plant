Our Story

Ankuram was born from a simple belief: that every home deserves a touch of nature. What started as a small greenhouse in the countryside has grown into a curated botanical destination for plant lovers across the country.

Our journey began in 2018 when our founder, Mira Sharma, transformed her family's unused greenhouse into a haven for rare and beautiful plants. With a background in horticulture and a passion for design, she set out to make plants accessible to everyone — not just as decorations, but as living art that transforms spaces and uplifts spirits.
