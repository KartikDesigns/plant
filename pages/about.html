Welcome to Ankuram

Est. 2026 — Growing beauty, one plant at a time.

A modern botanical haven dedicated to curating premium plants for every home. Authentic. Sustainable. Yours.

Why I Built This

A Passion That Grew Into a Mission

I built Ankuram because I believe that bringing plants into our homes changes everything — the way we breathe, the way we feel, the way we connect with ourselves.

It started in my small apartment balcony — a few pots of Tulsi, a struggling Snake Plant, and an unshakable curiosity. Every leaf that unfurled, every new root that took hold, felt like a small miracle. I realised that if a tiny balcony could hold so much life and joy, then everyone deserved to experience that magic.

Ankuram isn't just a plant shop — it's my way of sharing that joy with you. Every plant is hand-picked, every pot is chosen with care, and every order carries a piece of my belief that nature heals, inspires, and transforms. Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or buying your very first pot, welcome home.

The Vision

Why This Website Was Built

Ankuram was born from a dream — to make the healing power of plants accessible to every home in India, with just a few clicks.

I noticed that while plant nurseries were abundant, finding healthy, premium-quality plants online was still a challenge. People wanted greenery in their homes but didn't know where to start — which plant to buy, how to care for it, or how to get it delivered safely. That gap inspired me to build this platform.

This website is more than an e-commerce store — it's a bridge between nature and modern living. Every feature, from the plant care guides to the curated collections, is designed to make plant parenthood joyful, simple, and accessible for everyone. Whether you're in a city apartment or a suburban home, Ankuram brings the garden to your doorstep.

I built this with love, late nights, and an unwavering belief that a greener world starts at home. Welcome to Ankuram — where every leaf tells a story.
